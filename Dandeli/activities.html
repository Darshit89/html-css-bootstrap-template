Activities in Dandeli

Dandeli Hotel Resort offers the following adventurous activities in its scenic background which certainly
will rejuvenate your body and soul.



Zorbing Ball

Zorbing is the recreation or sport of rolling downhill
inside an orb, generally made of transparent plastic.
Zorbing is generally performed on a gentle slope, but
can also be done on a level surface, permitting more
rider control. In the absence of hills some operators
have constructed inflatable, wooden or metal ramps.

Natural Jacuzzi Bath

When Dandeli offers most of the things which are
natural, Jacuzzi is one where you get the natural
massage therapy by mother nature. As mother kali
starts spilling out of Supa Dam, she rushes wildly in the
midst of forest hitting the rocks and starts hitting
harshly.

Bird Watching

Dandeli forest is famous for nesting birds. There are
about 300 species of birds in and around Dandeli, they
include migrated birds eve. Dandeli Jungle is a home for
4 species of hornbills out of world’s 9 species. More
existence of hornbills in this vicinity indicates the
presence of thick forest.

Kayaking

Kayaking is another one of the great water sports that
Dandeli has in store. This exciting activity is conducted in
the Supa reservoir, an expansive man-made reservoir.
You can also camp in seclusion since the entire area is
relatively un-inhabited. This involves the use of a kayak
for moving across water.
