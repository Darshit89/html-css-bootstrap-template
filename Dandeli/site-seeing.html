Dandeli Site Seeing



DUDHSAGAR FALLS

The charming exception in the land of beaches, Goa,
Dudhsagar Falls offers a refreshing sight to behold and a
rejuvenating experience to encounter.

Timings: 9 am to 6 pm
Distance from Dandeli Bus Stand: The waterfall is 91 km
away from Dandeli Bus Stand.

SUPA DAM

Located in the ‘Garden City of India’, Karnataka, Supa Dam is the second biggest dam of the state built across the Kali River. Not only is it home to a scenic environment but also to the generation of hydroelectricity power

Timings: Tourists can plan their visit to Supa Dam during the day time.
Distance from Dandeli Bus Stand: Supa Dam is situated at a
distance of 21.8 km from Dandeli Bus Stand.

SYNTHERI ROCKS

Syntheri Rocks located within the Dandeli Forest, is a
combination of astonishing topography and abounding natural
beauty. These volcanic rocks are named after Ms. Cinthera, an
English lady, who discovered it..

Timings: 8:30 am to 5:00 pm
Distance from Dandeli Bus Stand: Dandeli Bus Stand is
situated at a distance of 31.6 km from Syntheri Rocks.

Crocodile Park

The Crocodile Park is a must-visit place for nature lovers who
love to explore the wildlife while indulging in adventurous
activities. This park is located in Dandeli and comes under
Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary.

Dandeli Crocodile Park, recognized by the Govt. of India, is a
natural wild crocodile park where you can notice many
crocodiles in the Kali River. It has no boundary so you may
notice crocodiles everywhere in the park.
