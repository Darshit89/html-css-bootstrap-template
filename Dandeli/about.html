The Story of Dandeli Luxury Resort , Elegant and
exclusive, Dandeli Luxury Resort invites you into its world
of greenery & nature.

Located in Dandeli, this exquisite jungle getaway rests
within the dense forest district of northern Karnataka, in
southern India.

Surrounded by a lush canopy of tropical rainforests, Dandeli Luxury Resort jungle resort is here to take you on a journey into nature’s wonderland – with a plethora of amphibians, birds, fish, mammals, reptiles and flowering plants, as your companions.
